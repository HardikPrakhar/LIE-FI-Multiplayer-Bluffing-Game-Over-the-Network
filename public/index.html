Bluff. Guess. Win.

How to Play

1. A question is shown to all players

2. Each player writes a fake answer

3. All answers are mixed with the real one

4. Players pick what they think is correct

5. Fool someone with your fake → you earn points 😈

6. Pick the correct answer → you earn points ✅

7. You can't vote for your own answer ❌

Scoring

+100
Correct answer
+50
Fool a player

Team Mode

The host can pair players into teams of 2. Teammates can private-chat during each round to coordinate their bluffs and votes. The host sets teams in the lobby before starting.

Teammates can't vote for each other's answers.
